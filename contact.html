Volunteer With Us

We're always looking for dedicated volunteers to help support our mission. If you're passionate about education, advocacy, and supporting the Eritrean community, we'd love to have you join our team.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Event planning and support
  • Tutoring and homework help
  • Translation and interpretation services
  • Administrative support
  • Mentoring parents in advocacy skills

To learn more about volunteer opportunities, please contact our Volunteer Coordinator at volunteer@eritreanparentsupport.org or call (555) 123-4567 ext. 3.
