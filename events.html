Events & Programs

Join us for workshops, conferences, camps, and other events designed to support Eritrean families and children.

Upcoming Events

June 15, 2023 | 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Parent Advocacy Workshop: Understanding Your Rights

Community Center, Room 201

This interactive workshop will help parents understand their rights within the educational system and learn effective strategies for advocating for their children. Topics include:

  • Legal rights of parents in the educational system
  • How to request and interpret educational assessments
  • Strategies for effective communication with educators
  • Building collaborative relationships with school staff

The workshop will be conducted in English with Tigrinya interpretation available.

July 10-14, 2023 | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Summer Enrichment Camp for Children

Riverside Elementary School

A week-long camp for children ages 6-12, featuring educational activities, cultural enrichment, and social development opportunities. Activities include:

  • Academic enrichment in reading, writing, and math
  • Cultural activities celebrating Eritrean heritage
  • Arts and crafts, music, and physical activities
  • Social skills development and team-building exercises

Space is limited to 30 children. Registration is required.

Register Now
July 10-11, 2023 | 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Annual Parent Conference: Empowering Advocacy

Community Conference Center

Our annual conference for parents runs concurrent with the children's summer camp. This two-day event features expert speakers, workshops, and networking opportunities. Topics include:

  • Keynote address: "Effective Educational Advocacy in a New Cultural Context"
  • Panel discussion: Parents sharing successful advocacy experiences
  • Workshops on special education services, school council participation, and supporting learning at home
  • Networking sessions with educators, advocates, and other parents

Conference registration includes lunch and materials. Childcare provided for children not attending the camp (ages 2-5).

Register Now

Back-to-School Community Gathering

City Park Pavilion

Join us for a community celebration as families prepare for the new school year. This event will feature:

  • Free school supplies for children
